About project

My Wallet is a personal finance tracking app designed to help users manage their income, expenses, and savings efficiently.

The app provides real-time insights, budgeting tools, and spending analytics to promote financial awareness and better money management.

Features

Check out the features, something unique and modern

    Expense & Income Tracking –Users can log daily transactions and categorize them.
    Budget Management - Helps set spending limits and track monthly expenses.
    Spending Analytics – Provides visual insights into financial habits through charts and reports.
    Recurring Payments & Reminders - Sends notifications for upcoming bills and subscriptions.
    Multi-Account Support – Allows tracking finances across multiple bank accounts.
    Secure & Private – Ensures user data protection with encryption and authentication.
